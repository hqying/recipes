Ingredients:
 - 2 potatoes
 - 50 to 100 ml milk
 - a little bit salt
 - a little bit butter

Directions:
 1. Cook potatoes with salt added until they're soft.
 2. Put potatoes into a big bowl and use a scoop or some special tool to squeeze potatoes with milk and melted butter. Meanwhile, put some salt in it depending on personal favor.
